Purchase Agreement for Community Tokens

Pact is a lightweight way for projects to raise money by issuing and selling a portion of project tokens along a bonding curve.

Why
Early community rounds often happen before the final equity, token, or revenue-sharing structure is obvious. Pact gives that round a simple placeholder: buyers fund the treasury now and receive community tokens that can represent future alignment.
What it is
It works like a small SAFE-style instrument for community tokens. The issuer specifies the cap table, raise target, maximum dilution, buyer allocations, and the discount curve for early buyers. The cap table is a 0xSplits Liquid Split; purchases are paid in USDC on Base.
How it works
  1. Create an issuance and deploy the Liquid Split-backed offering.
  2. Set buyer allocation sizes and send each buyer a unique purchase link.
  3. Buyers connect a wallet, purchase their allocation, and receive tokens.
  4. The project treasury receives funds once the minimum is met.
